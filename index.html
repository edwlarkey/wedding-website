Our Story

We grew up in such different places and so far from each other but met in neighboring states at a perfect time in our lives.

I, Natasha, grew up in Far East of Russia where I graduated from college with my Bachelor’s degree in Finance. Edward grew up in Missouri and received his Bachelor’s in Anthropology. Meanwhile my sister Vika got married and moved to the States with her husband Chris, and then asked me to come and live with her for awhile. I couldn’t miss the chance and gladly came to study for my Master’s in Marketing. During this time of my life I fell in love with the country and its people.

Edward started his great career at Healthport in Kansas City and enjoyed his time with family and best friends.

Later, I had to go back to Russia where I started career at Radisson hotels in Sochi, but my heart was always here.

So in 2 years I came back to work for a wonderful small church in Omaha, All Holy Spirit Church, where a lot of my great friends have been waiting for me and supported me every step of the way. This church made me grow as a person and showed me examples of humanity and miracles of God. My Thanksgiving present was unbelievable man whose name is Edward and whom I met online. He was celebrating the holiday with his parents in New York when our lives finally connected!! The rest is history and now we are happily getting married!

We are so happy to see all of you at our wedding!

We drove...A lot!
